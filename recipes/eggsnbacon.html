Eggs and Bacon

Description

This breakfast is referred to as "heart attack eggs" due to the high cholesterol content of the meal. As with anything in life, moderation is key with the heart attack egg breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 6 Slices of Bacon
  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 Pieces of White, Wheat, or Rye Bread
  • Salt, Black Pepper, and Garlic Powder To Taste
  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter

Steps

  1. Step 1: Put slices of bread in to toast until golden brown.
  2. Step 2: Fry the bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until crisp. Remove from the pan, and set on paper towels to drain.
  3. Step 3: Crack the eggs in to the pan with the bacon grease so that they are about 1 inch apart. Season with salt and pepper. When the eggs look firm, flip them over, and cook on the other side to desired doneness. Transfer to plate.
  4. Step 4: Butter the toast and transfer to plate with eggs.
  5. Step 5: Transfer bacon to plate and serve with eggs and toast.
